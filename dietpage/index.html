Japanese Macaque Diet!

-Japanese macaques are omnivores who eat anything ranging from fruits and flowers to insects and bird eggs. (NEPC)

-Japanese macaques are known to wash their food. For example, researchers once observed a macaque rinsing off a sweet potato in a freshwater stream. (NEPC)

-Japanese macaques climb trees to get the most energy sufficient leaves. (Animalia)

-These primates have a seasonal diet with mostly fruits in the summer and flowers and nectar in the winter. (ADW)
