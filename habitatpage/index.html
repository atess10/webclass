Japanese Macaque Habitats!

-Japanese macaques live in islands all across Japan that span a wide range of climates. They hold the title of “world’s northernmost wild populations of all non-human primates.” (NEPC)

-Japanese macaques live on elevations ranging from ground level to 4,921 feet. (NEPC)

-At night, Japanese macaques sleep in deciduous trees to avoid snowfall. (Animalia)

-Japanese macaques in less snowy southern environments weigh less than their northern counterparts. (ADW)
